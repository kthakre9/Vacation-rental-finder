Description

The Space

Beautiful Victorian home recently renovated within walking distance to Congress St, Longfellow Sq, Monument Sq, Commercial St, Old Port and lots of great restaurants including Hot Suppa, Pai Men, Petite Jacqueline, as well as numerous antique and thrift/vintage shops.

The building is located on Cumberland Avenue, in the Parkside Neighborhood of Portland. The bedroom is in our third floor condo.
The spacious room includes a Queen bed, down pillows, freshly laundered linens and towels. The room is cleaned after every guest. WIFI available!

We would love to try to accommodate last minute bookings and late arrivals. Contact us to see what we can do!

PARKING: Street Parking (day and overnight is fine, no residential sticker or in-state plate needed). A friendly reminder to read the signs for street cleaning.

Looking for a fun daytime activity? We suggest renting bikes at Gorham Bike & Ski (693 Congress St, 4 blocks away). Bike to local breweries, the beach, the Eastern Prom to have a picnic, Fort Williams Park to see the most famous light house and catch the food truck with the best Lobster Rolls in Maine.

We only accept reservations from people with completed profiles including an email address and phone number. Don't have an active U.S. phone number? Please let us know how we can best communicate with you.

Getting around
